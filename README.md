## About Me

I'm a Computer Engineering student at **Universitat Pompeu Fabra** in Barcelona and an **Automation Developer Intern at BancSabadell Zurich**.

My work sits at the intersection of software engineering, automation and product thinking. I like taking messy real-world problems and turning them into systems that are measurable, maintainable and actually useful.

Right now I'm focused on:

- **iOS development** with Swift, SwiftUI and offline-first local data.
- **Automation and AI agents** with n8n, Python, LLMs, APIs and custom workflows.
- **Web scraping and data pipelines** for lead generation, job search and operational intelligence.
- **FinTech and quantitative analysis** with Python, Pandas, backtesting and risk metrics.
- **SEO and GEO**: technical SEO, structured data, content architecture and AI-search visibility.

### LinkedIn Jobs Intelligence Bot

Automation system that collected around **10,000 LinkedIn job offers in 3 hours** and used an **n8n + LLM workflow** to prioritize the roles where my CV had the strongest match.

- Python scraping and data cleaning.
- Job title, company, location, seniority, stack and description extraction.
- CV-to-job matching using LLMs.
- Prioritized opportunity ranking to reduce job-search noise.

### [FadeChain](https://github.com/jordilleopart/fadechain)

Winner project at a **UPF blockchain hackathon**. FadeChain is a private voting system on **Ethereum Sepolia** using zero-knowledge proofs.

- Solidity smart contracts.
- zk-SNARKs and Groth16.
- Merkle trees for voter eligibility.
- Nullifier hashes to prevent double voting.
- Time-weighted voting mechanism.
